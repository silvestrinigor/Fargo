● Here's the analysis:                                                                                                        
                                                                                                                              
  ---                                                                                                                         
  Architecture                                                                                                                
                                                                                                                              
  Clean 4-layer stack:                                                                                                        
  IEngine → AuthenticationManager → AuthenticationClient → FargoSdkHttpClient                                                 
  Each layer has a clear responsibility: the engine bootstraps, the manager orchestrates and throws exceptions, the client    
  translates to/from HTTP, and the HTTP client handles raw requests.                                                          
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
  ---                                                   
  What's Good
             
  - Automatic token refresh scheduled 2 min before expiry is solid
  - Exception hierarchy is clean after the recent cleanup                                                                     
  - Logging uses LoggerMessage.Define (no allocation per call)
  - Lock on refreshTimer disposal is correct                                                                                  
  - Public surface is intentionally minimal (IEngine + IAuthenticationManager)
                                                                                                                              
  ---                                                   
  What's Missing (likely future scope)                                                                                        
                                                                                                                              
  The SDK only has Authentication right now. IEngine has no other managers — the architecture is clearly designed to grow
  (e.g., a UserManager, ArticleManager, etc.) but none exist yet.                                                             
                                                        
  ---                                                                                                                         
  The most important things to fix before real use: the silent refresh failure, the ChangePasswordAsync rename, and making the
   exception types consistently public.
